The maker

Started as a hobby. Still mostly is.

Parletta picks up stones the way some people collect postcards — a little at a time, whatever catches her eye — and turns them into necklaces and earrings at the kitchen table.

There's an Etsy shop too, but it doesn't get much attention these days. This page is really just a place to show off some of the pieces she's made, since a picture does a much better job than a description ever could.

"Nothing's mass-produced here — if it's a little uneven, that's just how the stone came."Parletta Erickson
